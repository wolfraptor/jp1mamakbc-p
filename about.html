JAKS Pharma Private Limited("JAKS") was founded by Jayant Kumar with aim to transform healthcare through cutting-edge pharmaceutical research, breakthrough treatments, and a commitment to patient well-being. We envision a world where no disease is incurable and every patient can live a healthier and happier life

Our core values serve as the foundation of our company culture and the guiding principles behind everything we do. Patient Centricity, Quality, Integrity, Innovation with Ethics, Global Reach through Innovation and last but not the least the human capital through Employee Development and Compassion. Encompassing all these, we are relentless in our pursuit of groundbreaking solutions. We embrace creative thinking, scientific curiosity, and a pioneering spirit to drive advancements in healthcare.

As we are all aware, patients are at the heart of our mission. Our priority is their needs, safety, and well-being in every decision we make, from research and development to product delivery. We uphold the highest ethical standards i.e. honesty, transparency, and compliance with regulations which are non-negotiable aspects of our identity in all our actions.

Success in healthcare requires a collaborative effort. We actively seek partnerships with like-minded organizations, healthcare professionals, and researchers to accelerate progress. As a startup, we recognize the importance of adaptability and agility. We are open to change, quick to respond to emerging opportunities, and nimble in our decision-making.



Mission and Vision

At JAKS our mission is to create global collaborations both in technology and products.

Our Mission Statement

To lead with integrity, innovate relentlessly, and collaborate widely to deliver patient-centric, sustainable solutions that drive positive change. We are dedicated to excellence in all we do, empowering individuals and organizations to thrive.

Our Vision Statement

Empowering a brighter future through innovation, integrity, and sustainability. We envision a world where our solutions positively impact lives, industries, and the environment, setting new standards for excellence.
